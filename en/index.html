Who is Tina?

She's been through it herself

Scientific preparatory classes at Lycee Saint-Louis, Master's in Actuarial Science at ISFA, then the Probability and Finance Master's at Ecole Polytechnique. Tina went on to work at Credit Agricole CIB and QBE. She knows the system from the inside, the good parts and the pitfalls.

Talking about academic guidance with your child is rarely simple. You want to help, but you don't always know how, and sometimes it creates more tension than clarity.
