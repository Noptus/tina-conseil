Privacy Policy

Tina Conseil is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to this site and the individuals we support, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the French Data Protection Act (loi Informatique et Libertes).

1. Data controller

Tina Conseil
Email: contact@tina-conseil.fr
Paris, France

2. Data collected

Personal data collected via the contact form includes:

  • First name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Profile (parent or student)
  • Free-text message describing your situation

3. Purpose of processing

This data is collected solely for the purpose of:

  • Contacting you to arrange a discovery call
  • Personalising the support offered
  • Managing the business relationship

No data is used for advertising purposes or shared with third parties.

4. Legal basis

Processing is based on your explicit consent, given via the checkbox on the contact form.

5. Data retention

Your data is retained for a maximum of 3 years from your last contact, unless otherwise required by law. You may request its deletion at any time.

6. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access: obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: request the deletion of your data
  • Objection: object to the processing of your data
  • Portability: receive your data in a structured format
  • Withdrawal of consent: withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: contact@tina-conseil.fr

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the CNIL (www.cnil.fr).

7. Cookies

This site does not use any tracking, advertising or analytics cookies. No third-party trackers are installed. Should this change in the future, a compliant consent banner will be implemented.

8. Security

Tina Conseil implements appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, modification, disclosure or destruction.

9. Data hosting

This site is hosted by GitHub, Inc. (United States). Contact form data is transmitted via a GDPR-compliant service (to be specified depending on the form solution chosen).

10. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices or regulatory requirements. The date of the last update is shown below.
